PROCESS



Metric 1 - Component Prioritization Workshop(Top Ranking)


To decide which components to include, I conducted a component prioritazation workshop.

Participants : 4 developers from SDLC

Participants are asked to

1. Cross out at least 2 sections that are unnecessary or unimportant.

2. Check up to 25 components that are most important to include in the first version of the design system.

3. Add a star by up to 5 important components that we should expect to spend extra effort getting right.

Metric 2 - Design Critique Workshop(Rose/Buds/Thorn)

After reviewing the design system, SDLC HCD group were asked to document their observations and opionions on sticky notes as positive, negative or having potential.

Team members were asked to sort their notes based on similarities.
