Givepulse.com Redesign - Capstone Project

Givepulse.com is a platform where you can find volunteer events and attendees. You can also use it to track your service hours, and stay organized with their volunteer management platform.

My job was to redesign and implement the pages that showcase individual volunteer experience and user profile.

My Process

1. Interact with existing teammates to determine how to improve and enhance existing designs.

2. Create and present wireframes and low fidelity prototype to test optional UX/UI designs.

3. Create and present a high-fidelity prototype.

4. Understand technical architecture and implement the web page design with PHP.
