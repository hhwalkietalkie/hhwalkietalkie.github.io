TenantDispute

A place to report and find out how to handle
the landlord or tenant problems.

UNDERSTAND

Target User:

1. Tenants
2. Landlords

 

Goals:

 

Provide a vent for tenants and landlord to complaint about bad customers.
Provide a place for tenants and landlord to communicate and find out how to handle problems about their customers.
Provide authoritative resource for tenants and landlords to take a reference and will not fall victim to the same person.

Problems:

What information need to be displayed?
What service should be provided?
How to make users trust this website?
What prompts would it give?

PROTOTYPE

Design Highlights:

On the first part of the page, directly ask users about their problems;




Give examples of what others are posting about. Remind users if they have the same problem;






Describe what they can do in this website to solve their landlord/tenant problem;







Remind users to select their identity when visit the site in order to see more adaptive content;




User tutorial as well as prompt for users to register;
