UNDERSTAND


IIImpact is a UX agency from Austin, Texas. During my internship over there I was given two projects. One is redesigning their website iiimpact.com. The other one is redesigning one of their in-house projects - tenantdispute.com.


Target User:

1. Tenants
2. Landlords

 

Goals:

 

Provide a vent for tenants and landlord to complaint about bad customers.
Provide a place for tenants and landlord to communicate and find out how to handle problems about their customers.
Provide authoritative resource for tenants and landlords to take a reference and will not fall victim to the same person.


Problems:

What information need to be displayed?
What service should be provided?
How to make users trust this website?
What prompts would it give?
